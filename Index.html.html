The Problem

A Broken System,
A Silent Crisis

84%
face at least
one barrier

NFHS-5 Data: 108,785 Women (Ages 15–49)

  • ~65% of India's women reside in rural areas — and their health remains deeply neglected.
  • Over half face facility and logistic barriers, reflecting systemic gaps in care.
  • Social taboos around PCOS, pregnancy, and menopause further limit awareness.
  • Current digital health apps ignore local dialects, have low-literacy interfaces, and complex bureaucratic processes for BPL beneficiaries.

Our Solution

Safe. Multilingual.
Stigma-Free.

"An AI-powered, multilingual women's health assistant that delivers safe, accessible, and stigma-free healthcare guidance for rural India."

01

Safe & Responsible AI

No medical diagnosis. Doctor escalation for serious symptoms. Harm-prevention built in at every layer.

02

Multilingual Accessibility

Supports all 22 Indian languages via IndicTrans2 by AI4Bharat. Simple, easy-to-understand responses in the user's native tongue.

03

Verified Information (RAG)

Retrieves trusted health data before generating answers. Actively reduces hallucinations and misinformation.

Llama 3

via Groq

Chosen to ensure scalability, privacy, and accessibility for rural India — where proprietary API costs would make the service unsustainable at scale.

Prompt Engineering

Programming the Personality

The system prompt shapes every interaction — ensuring safe responses, adapting to rural users' needs, and preventing misuse in a sensitive healthcare context.

# Ashvastree System Prompt (simplified) You are Ashvastree, a compassionate women's health assistant serving rural Indian women. - Use simple, clear language - Do not provide medical diagnoses - If symptoms seem serious, advise visiting a doctor - Be sensitive when discussing pregnancy, PCOS, menopause - Provide safe, general health guidance only

RAG Pipeline

How the AI Retrieves Before It Answers

RAG separates Ashvastree from a generic chatbot — it actually knows domain-specific women's health information, grounded in verified sources.

1
User Query

User sends a message in any Indian language (e.g. "PCOS symptoms?")

2
Translation

IndicTrans2 translates to English — 22 Indian languages supported

3
Embedding

Query converted to vector embeddings (text → numbers encoding meaning)

4
Similarity Search

Supabase finds top 3–5 closest matching verified health documents

5
Generation

Llama 3 generates a grounded, safe answer using retrieved context

6
Response

IndicTrans2 translates back — user receives safe answer in native language
